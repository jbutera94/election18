Summit Prep

Summit Preparatory Charter High School, founded in 2003, prides itself on its reputation as one of the best public schools in the nation. This tuition-free school in Redwood City, California focuses on preparing its students for success through developing emotional, social and intellectual skills through their shared six core characteristics: courage, compassion, curiosity, integrity, respect and responsibility.

Graduates from Summit Prep complete four-year college degrees at twice the national average: last year in 2018, 100 percent of the graduating class received acceptances to at least one four-year college or university.
